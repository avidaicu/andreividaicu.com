NSW Design System

  • By Andrei Vidaicu
  • September 30, 2023

The NSW Design System provides the essential building blocks to building consistent and scalable digital products and services for the NSW government.

Part of an enterprise-level SCRUM agile group, the Tech-Enablement team consists of a delivery manager, two developers and myself the UX/UI designer.

We are a hard working and passionate bunch, dedicated to delivering exceptional custmer service to over 45+ product teams that adopt the NSW DS.

My role (in a nut shell) -

  • Maintaining and supporting the NSW DS Figma Libary.
  • Engaging with the community to share and learn across our different channels.
  • Collaborating closely with child NSW gov design systems and aligning with the brand guidelines to ensure consistency, efficiency, and scalability in the design process.
  • Evaluating new design system ideas.
  • Designing and writting documentation for components.

Reflecting on lessons learned

  • Balancing customer and system needs is the real challange.
  • Prioritize as a team, learn from our customers, evaluate our assumptions.
  • Keeping it simple, prioritize accessibility for all, and cater for a diverse set of user needs.
